The Problem

Crisis decisions are made in silos.
We built a system where AI agents argue
so humans can decide better.

  • Health experts prioritize containment at any cost
  • Economic advisors push to keep businesses open
  • Safety officials worry about civil unrest and morale
  • No single perspective is correct — trade-offs are inevitable
